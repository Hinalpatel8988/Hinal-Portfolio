About

An 'about me' is a specific page on a website, usually a portfolio or personal website, which illustrates who the website is about, and it can serve as the reader's first impression of you. It allows visitors who come to your website to understand you better, helping you to develop a bond with them. By writing a successful 'about me' page, you can help to increase the number of visitors that stay on the site and then convert them into customers.

“About me” pages have the ability to engage and inform your site visitors in a personal and friendly way. For web professionals, our “About me” page can be critical in establishing a true connection with potential clients, and it can set us apart from a sea of other designers and developers. For different types of websites, keep in mind that the About page could be structured differently. For example, an About page for a blog or news site can be vastly different when compared to the same page for a portfolio website.
